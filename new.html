How It Works

Step 1

Enter your parking fine details and select the installment plan that works for you.

Step 2

Step 3

Receive confirmation of your payment and start paying off your parking fine over time.

FAQs

  • Can I pay off my parking fine in full?

    Yes, you can pay off your parking fine in full at any time without any penalty.

  • How many installments can I choose?

    You can choose to pay your parking fine in 2, 3, or 4 installments.

  • What happens if I miss a payment?

    If you miss a payment, you will be charged a late fee. However, we will work with you to help you get back on track.
